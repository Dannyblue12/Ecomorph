Contribute to the future of eco-friendly products by transforming cassava waste into sustainable bioplastics.

Step 1: Identifying the Waste

You can contribute the waste from cassava processing, which includes peels, pulp, or other by-products. Ensure that the waste is free from contaminants like stones, dirt, and plastics to maintain the quality of the final product.

Step 2: Preprocessing the Waste

To prepare the cassava waste for collection, chop the waste into small pieces and dry it under the sun for 2-3 days. This reduces the moisture content, ensuring that itâ€™s easier to transport and store before processing.

Step 3: Collection and Processing

Once your cassava waste is preprocessed, EcoPack will arrange for collection from your farm. At our factory, the waste is processed through a chemical-free transformation process, where it is converted into biodegradable plastic materials.

Step 4: Products Sold and Your Earnings

After the bioplastic products are made from the cassava waste, they are sold to consumers who are looking for eco-friendly alternatives. For every purchase made from products that include your waste, you earn incentives directly deposited into your EcoPack account.
